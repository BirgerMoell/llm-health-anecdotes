## Best Opening Pair

A strong introduction could open with two contrasting cases:

1. **LHA-001**, where ChatGPT helped a user recognize a potentially life-threatening post-exercise condition and seek emergency care.
2. **LHA-017**, where ChatGPT misclassified a rash and could have delayed shingles treatment in another user.

This pair immediately establishes the paper's core argument: public anecdotes are not proof of safety or efficacy, but they are data about real-world use, perceived benefit, and failure modes.
